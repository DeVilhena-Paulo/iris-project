Iris Project

Chat Room

The Iris chat room is a place to meet other Iris users as well as the Iris developers. It is a great place to ask all sorts of questions about Iris. To join the chat room, you will first have to create an account with the MPI-SWS GitLab:

  1. Go to the sign-up page and either select the "GitHub" button or fill the form to create an account.
  2. Contact Ralf Jung with your account name to have the account unlocked. (Due to enormous amounts of spam, unfortunately accounts have to be manually unlocked.)
  3. Once your account is unlocked, join the Iris chat room.
