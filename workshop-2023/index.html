Iris Workshop 2023

22 May – 26 May 2023, MPI-SWS, Germany

Program Location Organizers Registration Practical information About Saarbrücken

The third edition of the Iris workshop will be held in Saarbrücken and it will be focused on Iris-related research to allow for in-depth talks and discussions. There will be a three-day long program with talks from Monday 22 May until Wednesday 24 May. Participants are encouraged to stay 1-2 days longer if they can, for more informal discussions and interaction.
